Who We Are

This website is operated by Foul Play, trading as foulplay.games. When this policy refers to "we", "us" or "our", it means Foul Play.

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at: hello@foulplay.games

What Data We Collect

When you buy the game:

  • Your email address — so we can send you your game code

We do not collect your name. Payment details (card numbers etc.) are handled entirely by Stripe and never seen by us.

When you opt in to our newsletter after playing:

  • Your email address — only if you voluntarily provide it

This is separate from your purchase and entirely optional.

Automatically when you visit our website:

  • IP address, browser type, pages visited, time on site — collected via Google Analytics
  • Browsing behaviour used for targeted advertising — collected via Meta (Facebook/Instagram), Google Ads and TikTok pixels

These are collected via cookies. See the Cookies section below.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies — small files stored on your device. We use:

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function (e.g. processing your purchase)
  • Analytics cookies — Google Analytics, to understand site usage (IP address, pages visited)
  • Advertising cookies — Meta Pixel, Google Ads and TikTok Pixel, used to deliver relevant ads and measure ad performance

You will be asked for your consent before advertising and analytics cookies are placed. You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies.

How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain purchase records (email address and associated game code) for one year from the date of purchase, after which they are deleted.

Newsletter email addresses are kept until you unsubscribe or ask us to remove you.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR (and GDPR for EU residents) you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Object — object to us processing your data for marketing purposes
  • Withdraw consent — where we rely on consent, you can withdraw it at any time
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format
To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@foulplay.games. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) — the UK's data protection regulator — at ico.org.uk.

International Transfers

Some of our third-party services (including Stripe, SendGrid, Google and Meta) may process your data outside the UK or European Economic Area. Where this occurs, these providers are required to ensure your data is protected to an equivalent standard, typically through Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the UK ICO.
